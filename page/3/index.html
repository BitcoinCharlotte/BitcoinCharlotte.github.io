Buy Bitcoin and Dollar Cost Averaging

Swan bitcoin is one of the best new ways to buy bitcoin by dollar cost averaging (DCA). What's cool about Swan Bitcoin is that you can choose to have the bitcoin sent directly to your own wallet without having to store it with them! This is the Bitcoin way!
