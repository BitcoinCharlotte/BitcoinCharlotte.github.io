Resources

The goal of this page is to provide links and local resources relevant to your bitcoin needs, without overwhelming you. Check back frequently for updates! For now, please enjoy these preliminary staples we frequently tell our members about:

  • Andreas Antonopoulos - Really, this guy is a master in the bitcoin world. Watch his videos!
  • Bitcoin.org - Begin learning about bitcoin here and enjoy exploring your wallet options.
  • Satoshi Nakamoto's White Paper - The original paper that started it all in 2008.
  • Bitcoin-Only.com - Dig into the bitcoin rabbit hole and consume all things bitcoin!
  • Lopp's Bitcoin Resources - Lived in the nearby Triangle area Mr. Lopp is a true cypherpunk, privacy advocate, and expert in the space. His resources page will let you dig even deeeper.
  • Bitlisten.com - Watch and listen to bitcoin transactions in real time!
